About Model UN

For our future leaders

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. This activity takes place at MUN conferences, which is usually organized by a high school or college MUN club.

  • Meet students from all over the world
  • Polish your public speaking skills
  • Become acquainted with diplomacy and international issues
  • Enhance your leadership skills

Testimonials

“Hands down, Model UN has honestly become one of the highlights of my entire high school career. I’ve had the opportunity to connect with amazing new people, discover more about myself, and learn about the world we live in. It’s something that has shaped me into the leader I am today, and I highly encourage everyone to attend conferences and bring out their inner diplomat!”

“I walked out of my first MUN Conference with a new perspective on the world. It was truly eye-opening, getting the opportunity to develop an understanding of other countries and the world itself. Not only did I grow as a delegate, but as an overall person. I developed my self-confidence, collaboration and public speaking skills, all while having a good time.”

“It’s absolutely worth it. For those who hope to take initiative, do great things in the future and make the world a better place for everyone, this is for YOU. You will meet the leaders of tomorrow, who you will get the chance to discuss and debate contemporary issues with, in hopes of improving the world. What could be better than the prospect of contributing to a better future?”
